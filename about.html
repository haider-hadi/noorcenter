About NOOR CENTER

Our Story


In the heart of Kabirwala, where tradition meets modern life, NOOR CENTER was born from a simple dream — to bring premium fashion and lifestyle choices closer to home. Founded by Chaudhary Haider Seoul, a visionary with a passion for style and quality, NOOR CENTER has become a beacon of elegance and sophistication in the region.


At NOOR CENTER, we believe that clothing is not just fabric — it’s a reflection of identity. It tells a story about who you are, where you come from, and the life you want to live. That’s why we carefully select every piece in our collection, ensuring it represents sophistication, comfort, and modern elegance.


Premium Fashion in Choperhatta

NOOR CENTER is a premium fashion boutique located in Choperhatta. We provide high quality garments for Men, Women & Kids. Our collection is specially curated with modern designs and premium fabrics, ensuring comfort, style, and durability.


We believe in offering the best shopping experience with exclusive designs and premium service. Our products are crafted with attention to detail and delivered with love.
